    one punch man Saitama

    September 5, 2020
    | Sewing | | Plush | | Choly Knight |

    Pattern by Choly Knight This time I made his clothes as part of the plush itself, a much easier choice and suitable for the jumpsuit. I added the pink neck by sandwiching the nude fabric between the head and body at the attaching stage, and then left it hanging until the plush was stuffed so that I could see the shape to hand embroider it on. I used a iron on transfer pencil to put the face design onto the reverse side, then basted a guide running stitch before using a 4 strand stem stitch.
